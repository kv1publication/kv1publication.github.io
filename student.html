WHAT OUR FANTASTIC STUDENTS SAY

My school, my pride

My school establishes 

A concrete foundation of knowledge,

And wisdom in our tender brain,

Which we gratefully acknowledge.


Experienced knowledge,

From experienced teachers,

Lead us from the shadow of illiteracy

To the light of intelligence.


A shoulder to hold,

An ear to lend,

Some time to spend,

Which we all tend.


Together we perspire,

Together we aspire,

Together we achieve,

Together we receive.


My magnificent teachers so kind,

But wrong doings they do mind.

Inculcated into us is not fear,

As they believe to a student,

School days are very dear.


Our schedules have changed,

Our teachers have changed,

Our classes have changed,

Our friends have changed.


But one thing we will live on forever,

The pride we had for our team,

And which gave us a very big dream.


This prestigious institution gives us a hand,

No matter where we land,

We are sure to scintillate,

We all are very fortunate.


Studying here is not just a pleasure,

But its truly a lifetime's treasure.


We are the titans,

We are smart,

We are loud,

We are proud.


No matter where we go,

Or how far we leave,

We will always be proud to say,

Kv 1 tambaram is my school.

Sowjanya
XI-C
